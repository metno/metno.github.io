Security clearance

As part of the public administration, some MET positions require a security clarence. Please read more about clarence at NSM Web pages, especially the frequently asked questions page - Google translated for your convenience.

Short summary: 1. Norwegian citizens are eligible for security clearance 2. Citizens of NATO countries, and citizens of countries Norway extends a great deal of trust are eligible for a security clearance 3. Citizens of countries where person inquiry responses does not in general meet with required standards of trust, an applicant might never obtain a Norwegian security clearance, or the clarence process may take several years to conclude 4. If investigations of your person record evaluated to not comply with clearance requirements you may be denied clarence regardless of your citizenship

If you belong to category 3 or 4, and the position require security clearance, we will discourage you from applying for the position.

If you belong to category 1 or 2 we encourage you to go forth with your application.
